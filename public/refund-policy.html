Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied. Here's what to expect.

Overview

All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Because payments are handled by these platforms, refunds are subject to their respective refund policies. This page describes our own commitment to your satisfaction alongside the platform rules.

Apple App Store

For purchases made on iOS, refund requests must be submitted directly to Apple. You can request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com. Apple's standard refund window is 14 days from purchase.

Google Play Store

For purchases made on Android, you may request a refund through the Google Play Store within 48 hours of purchase. For requests outside that window, contact Google Play support or reach out to us directly.

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

If you are unsatisfied with a Pro subscription (monthly or annual) for any reason within 7 days of your first payment, contact us at libritania95@gmail.com and we will do our best to resolve the issue or assist you in obtaining a refund through the appropriate platform.

We cannot directly process refunds ourselves — all billing is managed by Apple or Google — but we will actively support your refund request and provide any documentation needed.

Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly Pro subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from your App Store or Google Play account settings. Cancellation prevents future charges but does not result in a prorated refund for the current billing period. You retain access to Pro features until the end of the paid period.

If you were charged after intending to cancel, contact us and we will help you request a refund from the platform.

Annual Subscriptions

Pro Annual subscriptions are billed as a single yearly charge. You can cancel at any time to prevent auto-renewal. If you cancel mid-year, you retain Pro access through the end of your paid period. Refunds for unused months on an annual plan are handled on a case-by-case basis — contact us within 7 days of the charge and we will do our best to assist you.

How to Request a Refund

To request support with a refund:

  1. Email us at libritania95@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request."
  2. Include your order ID or purchase receipt from the app store.
  3. Briefly describe the reason for the refund request.
  4. We will respond within 2 business days.
