Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel your Pro subscription at any time from the app settings or your app store account. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

What is the difference between Monthly and Annual?
Both plans include exactly the same features. Annual billing saves you ~33% compared to paying month to month ($63.99/year vs $96.00/year).

Does the Starter plan show ads?
Yes. The free Starter plan is supported by ads. Upgrading to any Pro plan removes all ads.

What payment methods are accepted?
Payments are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store using your existing payment method on file.

Do prices include taxes?
Prices shown are before any applicable taxes. Your app store may add VAT or sales tax depending on your region.
